# Chatbot Simulation Questions Guide

Here are some common questions users might ask the AI assistant about the Ant Colony Optimization (ACO) virus simulation:

## Understanding the Simulation Basics
1. **What is happening in this simulation?**
2. **What do the green, yellow, and red dots represent?**
3. **What is the goal of the ants in this network?**
4. **How does the malware spread?**

## The ACO Algorithm (Ant Colony Optimization)
1. **What is Ant Colony Optimization (ACO)?**
2. **How does the "Pheromone weight" (Alpha) parameter affect the ants?**
3. **What happens if I increase the "Heuristic weight" (Beta)?**
4. **What does the "Evaporation rate" (Rho) mean in this context?**
5. **Why do the paths between nodes become thicker or thinner?**

## Interpreting the Metrics
1. **How is the "Net resolved events" metric calculated?**
2. **What does the "Cycle containment rate" mean?**
3. **How is "Algorithm Efficiency" evaluated?**
4. **Why is the total pheromone mass constantly fluctuating?**

## Experimenting with Parameters
1. **What is the best configuration to stop a high malware emission rate?**
2. **If I increase the number of agents (ants) to maximum, will they immediately stop the virus?**
3. **What happens if I set the pheromone evaporation rate to 1.0 (100%)?**
4. **How can I make the ants react faster to new, unexpected infections?**

## The Threat Intelligence Mechanics
1. **How do ants share threat intelligence with each other?**
2. **Can ants move to a healthy node, or do they only trace infected ones?**
3. **Why do some ants cluster together while others explore randomly?**
4. **How exactly does an ant "cure" a degraded or infected node?**
